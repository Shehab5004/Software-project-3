Step of how to open facebook account

  1. Go to the Facebook website or app
    • Visit facebook.com in your browser or download the Facebook app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Click on "Create New Account":
    • On the Facebook homepage, click the "Create New Account" button.
  3. Enter your personal details:
    • Fill in your first name and last name.
    • Enter your mobile number or email address.
    • Create a password for your account.
  4. Enter your birthdate:
    • Select your birth date from the dropdown menus.
  5. Select your gender:
    • Choose from the available options (male, female, custom).
  6. Click "Sign Up":
    • After filling out the form, click the "Sign Up" button to proceed.
  7. Verify your email or phone number:
    • Facebook will send a confirmation code to the email address or phone number you entered. Check your inbox or SMS, and enter the code to verify your account.
